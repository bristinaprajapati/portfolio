BLOGS

Blogging can be a great way for students to reflect on their experiences and share their thoughts and ideas with a wider audience. Writing a blog every week as a computer science student during the first semester was challenging but rewarding experience. The process of writing my blog allowed me to deeply reflect on my learning and gain a deeper understanding of the subject matter. Not only did it enhance my technical writing skills, but it also gave me a sense of fulfillment to know that my ideas and experiences could help and inspire others.
